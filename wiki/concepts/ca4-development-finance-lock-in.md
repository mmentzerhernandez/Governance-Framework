# CA4 — Development-Finance Conditionality & Vendor Lock-In

Capability-area hub. How a government accepts foreign-financed digital infrastructure **without**
surrendering sovereignty to a single vendor or donor.

## The instruments
- [[india-dpi-stack]] — the open-standards, government-owned architecture (basis of the DPI Test).
- [[undp-dpg-standard]] — a 9-criteria vetting standard + registry of open digital public goods.
- [[green-climate-fund]] — direct-access + pooled-finance + conditionality-limitation analogue.

## Package component drawn from CA4
The [[infrastructure-safeguard]] — the DPI Open-Standards Test — is the **Protect**-phase screen for
incoming foreign development-finance proposals. The cross-cutting mechanism is detailed in
[[vendor-lock-in-dpi-test]].
