# CA3: AI Harm Reporting, Redress & Accountability

Capability-area hub. A **minimum-viable redress system** needs only three things: a complaints
channel, a documented explanation right, and a human-review escalation path, all achievable within
an existing ombudsman or consumer-protection mandate.

## The instruments
- [[oecd-ai-incidents-monitor]]: policy-oriented incident classification (the 4-category taxonomy).
- [[ai-incident-database-nist]]: case-level technical detail + NIST risk-management framework.
- [[eu-ai-act]]: high-risk obligations reusable as a procurement-contract standard (Article 26).
- [[world-bank-procurement]]: digital/AI contract clauses (audit rights, exit planning).

## Package component drawn from CA3
The [[redress-focal-point]] (a small national incident point using the OECD taxonomy) is the
**Anchor**-phase redress endpoint.
