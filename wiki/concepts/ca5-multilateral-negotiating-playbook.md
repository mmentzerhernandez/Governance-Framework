# CA5: Shifting Multilateral Negotiating Language

Capability-area hub and the target of the **Engage** phase. This is also the *multilateral-negotiating-playbook* referenced from the landing sequence.

## The precedents
- [[doha-declaration]] (2001): rewriting treaty interpretation with domestic impact data + a single
  shared coalition text + human-rights reframing.
- [[nagoya-protocol]] (2010/2014): making benefit-sharing binding via domestic law as leverage and
  intersessional coalition maintenance.
- [[cbdr-rc-paris]]: defending a Global South principle for 30 years via principle-bundling and the
  nationally-determined (NDC) architecture.
- [[global-digital-compact]] (2024): the first AI text substantially shaped by the G77.

## The transferable playbook
Aggregate domestic impact evidence (from the [[protection-survey]] + [[diagnostic-anchor]]); assemble
a single shared coalition draft (G77/AU/CARICOM/ASEAN); reframe as a right-to-development issue;
identify existing "equitable access" textual footholds; maintain an intersessional coordination group.
