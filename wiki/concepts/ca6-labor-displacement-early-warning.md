# CA6 — Labor-Displacement Early-Warning Systems

Capability-area hub, feeding the **Monitor** phase. Early-warning indicators are most reliable at
the **sectoral occupation level**, not economy-wide.

## The instruments
- [[ilo-automation-methodology]] — task-level automation-exposure index, with developing-country data.
- [[world-bank-step]] — household skills survey capturing informal-sector exposure + ID4D exclusion.
- [[kenya-finaccess]] — the operational model for survey-embedded AI-exclusion questions.

## Package component drawn from CA6
The [[protection-survey]] — 5–7 questions in an existing national survey, paired with the ILO AEI —
is the monitoring instrument.

> 🔍 Gap: No Global South country has yet deployed a *comprehensive* AI-specific labor-displacement
> early-warning system — only sector-specific monitoring exists. This is the least mature capability
> area in the report.
