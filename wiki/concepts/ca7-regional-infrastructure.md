# CA7 — Regional Shared-Services & Pooled Digital Infrastructure

Capability-area hub. Regional pooling offers cost efficiencies (collective procurement, shared
platforms) but requires patience — these are the furthest-advanced examples.

## The models
- [[smart-africa]] — the most operationally advanced Global South body: cloud marketplace, blended
  fund, MOU template (30–40% cloud savings).
- [[asean-defa]] — the first comprehensive regional digital-economy treaty among developing economies
  (modular 8-pillar architecture).
- [[caricom-cdtp]] — small-island-state pooling with qualified-majority governance (28% tender
  savings).
- [[gaia-x]] — a cautionary federated-cloud model: consensus governance and vendor influence are
  what *not* to replicate.

## Connection to the package
Pooling is not one of the six package components but reinforces the [[infrastructure-safeguard]] —
collective procurement strengthens each member's bargaining power against lock-in, and is the
institutional home for the **Engage** phase's regional coordination.
